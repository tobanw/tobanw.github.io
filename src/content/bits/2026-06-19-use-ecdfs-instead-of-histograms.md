I just published a new essay: [Use eCDFs instead of histograms](/writing/use-ecdfs-instead-of-histograms/).

The basic argument is that eCDFs are almost always a better default for visualizing distributions: no bin-width fiddling, easy percentiles, and much better behavior when you zoom the x-axis without wanting to forget about the tails.
